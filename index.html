What actually is MOBA?

MOBA refers to games which generally have a very specific set of features in their gameplay and design. Basically, such titles tend to have a real-time strategy element and revolve around a fairly simple aim: to compete in a team against opponents and defeat them in a form of battle.

Choose champions according to your playstyle weather faster than anything or maybe not so fast but sure do huge damage or has the ability to control magic itself.
